Directions to Stanford University

From Highway 280 North & South:
Exit Sand Hill Road & go east toward Stanford.  Continue past Pasteur and Vineyard, turning right at Arboretum Rd.  Go past Quarry and turn right on Palm Dr.  At the end of Palm Dr. is the Oval.

From Highway 101 North & South:
Take University Ave. south thru Palo Alto.  At El Camino it changes name to Palm Drive and ends at the Oval.
