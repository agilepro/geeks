Stanford University<p>
<b>Directions:</b><br>
From Highway 280 North & South:<br>
Exit Sand Hill Road & go east toward Stanford.&nbsp; Continue past Pasteur and Vineyard, turning right at
Arboretum Rd.&nbsp; Go past Quarry and turn right on Palm Dr.&nbsp; At the end of Palm Dr. is the Oval.&nbsp;
Park anywhere that it's legal.<p>
From Highway 101 North & South:<br>
Take the Embarcadero Road exit west toward Stanford.&nbsp; At El Camino Real, Embarcadero turns into Galvez
Street as it enters the university.&nbsp; The next right is Arboretum Rd.&nbsp; Go two blocks and turn left on
Palm Dr.&nbsp; At the end of Palm Dr. is the Oval.&nbsp; Park anywhere that it's legal.&nbsp; Parking is free after 4 pm.<p>
I suggest that you take map printouts at zoom levels 2 & 4, as it can be easy to get lost.<p>
