The Day Columbo Retired

 

By VMF-214

 

 

Lt. Columbo: All right sir, lemme see if I got this straight: You say you were made by a Creator to look and be just like Him, is that it?

 

Witness: Yes Lieutenant, how many times do I have to tell you?

Lt. Columbo: OK, so you got a mind and a brain just like He does?

Witness: Yes, of course, mine works just like His, but His is much bigger. See, He’s perfect in every way, He doesn’t make any mistakes, and He knows everything. But that’s not the best part – the best part is, he loves me, and I can’t tell you what a warm glow I get from that.

Lt. Columbo (squints and takes a puff of cigar): Now, you say you were using that brain and ….

Witness: I used it a LOT, because He is always telling me not to squander the talents He gave me and I spent a lot of time with some other folks who were using theirs too

Lt. Columbo: And you say you discovered He couldn’t possibly exist?

Witness: Yes

Lt. Columbo: When did you discover this?

Witness: It was about 3 in the morning a couple weeks ago.

Lt. Columbo: Ok, so He gave you a brain, you used the brain to figure out He wasn’t real—so what, sir, if I may ask, is exactly the problem?

Witness: Threats

Lt. Columbo (raises eyebrow): What kind of threats?

Witness: He said He was going to pour gas on me and set me on fire in front of my mother and wife and kids while they’re all at a party at His mansion whooping it up.

Lt. Columbo: I know, we got the call down at the station. We’re going to put a tail on Him and…

Witness: WAIT! Don’t do that!

Lt. Columbo: And why not, sir?

Witness: Cause there’s an out

Lt. Columbo: What do you mean an out?

Witness: He’s got a relative who is actually the same person as Him. He told me He arranged to have the relative bumped off, but see, the relative was only bumped off for a couple of days, then he wasn’t bumped off any more, He did this all for me – like I said, He loves me -- and if I bought this, and also ate a piece of the relative every now and then, He said he would forget all about the gasoline thing. So, I’m thinking about it.

Lt. Columbo (squinting heavily): Ok, sir, let’s add this all up: You were made by Mr. Perfect, He gives you the brain that’s a copy of His, then He threatens to set you on fire for using the brain, even though He loves you,  then He changes His mind, has Himself bumped off to fix the mistake that He made by making you, even though you say He doesn’t make mistakes, only He knows ahead of time He’s not really bumped off, and even though the relative didn’t do anything wrong and He thinks you did, if you buy this story, and eat the relative, then you don’t get set on fire, is that right?

Witness: That’s about it, yes.

Lt. Columbo (shakes head slowly): OK sir, here’s my card, you be sure and call us if you hear from Him again.

(Turns and heads for door, then suddenly turns around)

Oh…sir..just one more thing?

Witness (looking peeved): YES Lieutenant?

Lt. Columbo: Well sir, it’s just…I mean…when did He tell you about this relative thing?

Witness: He didn’t tell me -- it’s all in a book I have at home

Lt. Columbo: A book?

Witness: That’s what I said – is there anything else?

Lt. Columbo:  Yes sir, how do you know the book is right?

Witness: BECAUSE, Lieutenant, it says right in the book that the book is right, and so did the guy who gave it to me.

Lt. Columbo (grins and takes a long puff of cigar): Well sir, I have to admit, for the first time… for the very first time -- I …am… stumped.

With that, Lt. Columbo took off for Florida and was never seen around here again.
