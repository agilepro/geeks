David Fitzgerald is a former Christian apologist who has been called "one of the busiest Atheist activists in the Bay Area." He is the Action Coordinator for San Francisco Atheists, and serves on the board for Center for Inquiry- San Francisco. He is founder of the annual Bay Area Darwin Day celebration "Evolutionpalooza" and Director of Student Outreach for the Garrison-Martineau Project, a non-profit organization that encourages dialogue between believers and nonbelievers. In addition, he works with Camp Quest West, a summer camp for Atheist youth. He is also the author of the forthcoming book "The 10,000 Christs and the Evaporating Jesus" examining the historical evidence for Christ. Dave has been a featured speaker for the last two American Atheist national conventions.<p>
Chang Yuon is a Christian apologist who teaches at Grace Church of Pleasanton. He has taught the youth how to defend the faith for over ten years. He was also the Youth Director at Korean Central Presbyterian Church from 2001-2005. From 2005-2007, Chang also served as the Youth Pastor at Hamonah Presbyterian Church in Concord. He lives in Dublin with his lovely wife Young and his one-year-old son Ryan.<p>
