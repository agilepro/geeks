Eternal Heavenly Servitude

by Diderot Leumas

I have often wondered about the Christian concept of heaven. The Christian concept of Hell is well illustrated with vividly terrifying images in books such as Revelation, Ezekiel, Daniel and most exquisitely horrifying of all, Dante’s Inferno. Conversely, as a Christian, are you really aware of what exactly heaven is like according to the Bible?


Many Christians may think that it is being in the presence of God, or being one with God. The first is partially correct but quite misleading. The second is clearly wrong if God’s words are to be taken literally as described in the biblical book of Revelation.


Take a moment and reflect. What do you think you will be doing FOR ETERNITY in heaven? Look inside you and consider what is it you WOULD LIKE to do FOR EVER in heaven. Do you really want to spend ETERNITY contemplating the visage of God and/or Jesus? Don’t you think it will become somewhat boring after a millennium or two, even if you were to keep alternating between the two and despite the hymnals?


According to Revelation, heaven is a bedazzling, bejeweled, golden replica of Jerusalem but without the wailing wall or the temple of Solomon or the church of the Holy Sepulcher and most definitely without the Dome Of The Rock. Also presumably without the ghettos and slums of the current city. So in reality it is not much of a replica. So why call it Jerusalem?


Why is God so obsessed with a city that was originally conquered from its rightful owners (the Jebusites Joshua 15:63) by ordering and aiding Joshua to genocide them (but failing mind you)?


Reflect on this for a moment: God had to order some men to kill other men and take their city from them so as to build him a house in it. Then he considers this man made house the ONLY place on the WHOLE EARTH to be his DWELLING in preference to the WHOLE UNIVERSE.


Now if you do not see this as the action of a RETARDED BLOODTHIRSTY GOD then you must agree that it is the writings of RETARDED BLOODTHIRSTY DESERT DWELLING EVIL RACISTS who are on par if not worse than the icons of evil and retardation called the Taliban. The Israelites according to what the Bible depicts them to be are the 21st Century BCE equivalent of the Taliban in the 21st century CE. Any one who believes that this myth is holy is sorely in need of a good wake up call.


Just as it is on earth, so it is in the city of heaven. Gentiles STILL are second-class citizens as far as Yahweh is concerned (see Romans 11:11-31 and of course the rest of the Bible). Additionally, there will still be “KINGS who bring their glory into the city” whatever that means in the after life!


The city is to have 12 gates on which will be written the names of the 12 children of Israel who are wrongly listed in Revelation (7:4-8 compare to Genesis 49:2-28, Exodus 1:1-6 and Numbers 1:5-15). Why does the city of heaven need twelve gates or walls altogether? Why are the 12 tribal names of the children of Jacob so darn important so as to be EXALTED above any other nations or people in the entire history of humanity? What did these desert-dwelling shepherds do that is so special so as to deserve their names to be written for ETERNITY on the 12 GATES of the ETERNAL HEAVENLY DWELLING PLACE of GOD?


Did they not sell their brother into slavery after trying to kill him (Genesis 37:18-29)? Didn’t Judah father two children with his daughter-in-law after contracting her as a prostitute (Genesis 38)? The entire Bible is nothing but a chronicle of their atrocities against sub-humans (gentiles) as well as each other; albeit at the behest and with the aid of Yahweh himself. Read Judges 19:23 to 21:25 for a TYPICAL Stomach Turning GRUESOME tail of the cruelty, bloodthirstiness, sexism and utter viciousness that Yahweh and his chosen people are capable of achieving. These are the tribes whose names are to be ETCHED FOR ETERNITY on the 12 useless gates of the CITY OF HEAVEN.


Notice how Yahweh does not think that a SINGLE gentile deserves to have his or (horror of horrors) HER name in an honored place. Not a single gentile nation either. People like Isaac Newton, Edward Jenner (Vaccine Inventor), Galileo Galilei, Marie Curie, Florence Nightingale and Louis Pasteur were all Jesus Loving Good Christians who raised humanity to a higher level of existence. Why do they not deserve at least a stone or brick named after them?
