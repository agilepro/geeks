From: Montgomery Advertiser

 

Letters show enlightenment

I would like to comment on three letters appearing in your July 17 edition. I applaud the letters from H. E. Harrison and Anthony R. Stojak. It is refreshing to know that there are citizens of Alabama who hold such enlightened views.

Our Constitution guarantees religious freedom, and that includes those who choose to follow no particular established religion. One has only to consider the dreadful Afghan-Taliban regime to see what can occur when government and religion are inseparable.

On the other hand, the letter from Cecil Park exemplifies appalling ignorance. His opinion, in mine, was anything but “humble.”

Mary Murrie
Eufaula
