To show the history of a particular stock, enter its symbol in the blank area and click on "Get it!".

 

 

To show the list of stocks in any of the ten stock groups, simply click on the appropriate line in the Info Center.
With a list of stocks displayed, to see the history of particular stock in that list is doing - click on its symbol.
To look at the next stock in the list, just hit the space bar or click on the blue right arrow at the top.
To look at the previous stock in the list, just click on the blue left arrow at the top.
