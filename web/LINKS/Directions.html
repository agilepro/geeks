Directions to the Atheists of Silicon Valley 7:30 PM Wednesday meetings, at 425 Lotus Lane, Mountain View:

From 101 (just north of where 85 ends) in Mountain View:

Take Shoreline south (right) about three blocks.
At the Safeway, take a right onto Montecito.
Follow the directions below, from Montecito to Lotus Lane.

From Central in Mountain View:

Take Shoreline north about one block.
At the second stop light, take a left onto Montecito.

From Montecito to Lotus Lane:

Go one long block to a stop sign.
Go one short block and turn right into a school parking lot, where Granada T's from the left.
Park and walk along Granada.
Lotus Lane is the first street on your left.
The clubhouse is on the right between 423 & 427 Lotus Lane, and the entrance is to the right.

Driving from the Mountain View CalTrain station:

Go west (right) on Evelyn and right on Castro.
Cross the RR tracks and take a left onto Central Expwy.
Take the next right onto Shoreline.
Take Shoreline north about one block.
At the second stop light, take a left onto Montecito.
Follow the directions above, from Montecito to Lotus Lane.

Walking from the Mountain View CalTrain station:

Go west (right) on Evelyn and right on Castro.
Cross the RR tracks and take a left on Jackson.
The next street is Stierlin, go right.
Cross Shoreline at the traffic light, and you are on Montecito.
Follow the directions above, from Montecito to Lotus Lane.
