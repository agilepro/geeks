<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>&lt;h3&gt;Questions
for Dr. Laura&lt;/h3&gt;<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>Dr.
Laura Schlessinger is a US radio personality who dispenses advice to<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>people
who call in to her radio show. Recently she said that<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>homosexuality
is an abomination according to Leviticus 18:22,<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>and
cannot be condoned under any circumstances. The<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>following
is an open letter to Dr. Laura penned by a US resident, which<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>was
posted on the Internet. It's funny, as well as informative.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>Dear
Dr. Laura:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>Thank
you for doing so much to educate people regarding God's Law. I have<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>learned
a great deal from your show, and try to share that knowledge with<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>as many
people as I can. When someone tries to defend the homosexual<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>lifestyle,
for example, I simply remind them that Leviticus 18:22 clearly<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>states
it to be an abomination. End of debate.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>I do
need some advice from you, however, regarding some of the other<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>specific
laws and how to follow them.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>1. When
I burn a bull on the altar as a sacrifice, I know it creates a<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>pleasing<span
style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>odor for the Lord -- Lev. 1:9. The problem
is my neighbors. They<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>claim
the odor<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>is not pleasing to them.
Should I smite them?<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>2. I
would like to sell my daughter into slavery, as sanctioned in Exodus<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>21:7.<span
style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>In this day and age, what do you think would
be a fair price for her?<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>3. I
know that I am allowed no contact with a woman while she is in her<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>period
of menstrual uncleanliness -- Lev. 15:19-24. The problem is, how do<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>I tell?
I have tried asking, but most women take offence.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>4. Lev.
25:44 states that I may indeed possess slaves, both male and<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>female,
provided they are purchased from neighboring nations. A friend of<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>mine
claims that this applies to Mexicans, but not Canadians. Can you<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>clarify?
Why can't I own Canadians?<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>5. I
have a neighbor who insists on working on the Sabbath. Exodus 35:2<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>clearly
states he should be put to death. Am I morally obligated to kill<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>him
myself?<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>6. A
friend of mine feels that even though eating shellfish is an<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>abomination
-- Lev. 11:10-- it is a lesser abomination than homosexuality.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>I don't
agree.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Can you settle this?<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>7. Lev.
21:20 states that I may not approach the altar of God if I have a<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>defect
in my sight. I have to admit that I wear reading glasses. Does my<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>vision
have to be 20/20, or is there some wiggle room here?<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>8. Most
of my male friends get their hair trimmed, including the hair<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>around
their temples, even though this is expressly forbidden by Lev.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>19:27.
How should they die?<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>9. I
know from Lev. 11:6-8 that touching the skin of a dead pig makes me<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>unclean,
but may I still play football if I wear gloves?<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>10. My
uncle has a farm. He violates Lev. 19:19 by planting two different<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>crops
in the same field, as does his wife by wearing garments made of two<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>different
kinds of thread (cotton/polyester blend). He also tends to curse<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>and
blaspheme a lot. Is it really necessary that we go to all the trouble<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>of
getting the whole town together to stone them? -- Lev.24:10-16.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>Couldn't
we just burn them to death at a private family affair like we do<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>with
people who sleep with their<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>in-laws?
(Lev. 20:14)<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>I know
you have studied these things extensively, so I am confident you<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>can
help. Thank you again for reminding us that God's word is eternal and<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>unchanging.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoPlainText><span style='mso-fareast-font-family:"MS Mincho"'>Your
devoted disciple and adoring fan.<o:p></o:p></span></p>
